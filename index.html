  15. Sichimi Togarashi

Process

1. Add ingredients for okonomiyaki batter :
Thinly Sliced Cabbage
Flour
Dashi
Salt
Sugar
Baking Powder
Sesame Oil
Eggs
2. Lay out pork belly strips on skillet (set on low/medium heat)
3. Pour batter onto skillet
If too hot, egg may burn
Use some oil to prevent sticking
4. Use spatula to help mold pancake into circle as the egg is setting
5. When the bacon naturally separates from the pan and the pancake is somewhat firm, flip the pancake
Use two spatulas if needed
6. When bottom is ready, set pancake on plate
7. Dress up okonomiyaki
Kewpie Mayo
Okonomiyaki Sauce
Konbu
Aonori
Sichimi Togarashi
Green Onion
